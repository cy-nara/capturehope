About Us

At capTure, we are committed to revolutionizing substance abuse recovery. Founded by Cyra Narahari, Architha Srinivasan, and Anthony Rodas-Camacho, capTure addresses the chronic issue of relapse with an innovative solution: a discreet, qEEG-enabled baseball cap that detects brain activity linked to cravings before conscious awareness.

Our mission is to empower individuals in their journey to long-term sobriety through early intervention, delivered seamlessly via a mobile app. By bridging cutting-edge technology with compassion, capTure offers hope and proactive support to those striving for a better future.
