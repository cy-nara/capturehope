About Us

Welcome to capTure

Substance use disorder (SUD) is a pandemic, infiltrating the very core of our society by etching deep scars on individuals, families, and societies. Only 15-20% of individuals stay abstinent one year after treatment, with relapse rates as high as 85%. Meet capTure: a revolutionary tool designed to empower individuals to embrace growth and make lasting, positive lifestyle changes.

By tapping into relative emotional triggers and brain wave patterns, our product helps users identify alarming data to prevent further substance use. capTure’s mobile app, ‘capTure.com’, seamlessly integrates advanced brain wave analysis to track and monitor users’ unique emotional and behavioral patterns, identifying early indicators of relapse. Our artificial intelligence extracts and relays critical data to healthcare professionals and loved ones while simultaneously offering personalized resources for maintaining abstinence.

To avoid social stigmatization, wireless EEG biosensors are embedded in a baseball cap to promote continuous use without individuals being defined by their condition. We employ a one-time cost for the designed cap with an additional monthly subscription accounting for the biosensor primer fluid and the in-app data processing software. capTure builds a stepping stone for victims of this pandemic, taking control of their journey and creating a healthier, more fulfilling life.

Our Design

capTure’s performance relies on its Brain-Computer Interface design and wireless EEG biosensor electrodes (Fp1, Fp2, T3, T4, F7, F8, F3, F4, and Pz), which measure potential differences using differential amplifiers and a mastoid reference electrode. A primer fluid is applied to lower electrode impedance for optimal signal quality, while the cap is designed for discreet, all-day use without drawing attention.

The word “capture” symbolizes our ability to detect and address key triggers through cutting-edge technology, while “hope” emphasizes the belief in ecovery, resilience, and growth. Together, the name encapsulates our commitment to blending innovation with compassion, offering users a transformative journey toward sustained well-being.
