Our Story

Substance use is highly normalized in our communities, particularly in youth culture and media. As high school students from Houston, Texas, and members of immigrant families, we have witnessed firsthand the dangerous consequences of substance use and seen relationships damaged by its cruelty. Inspired by professional and personal experiences, we were motivated to innovate an alternative to existing treatments for substance use disorder. Our goal is to not only revolutionize the approach to treating this condition but also collaborate with professionals to combat the stigma surrounding it.

Our Vision

To create a world where technology in substance use disorder treatment radically reduces rates of relapse and works to destigmatize the condition.

Our Mission

To revolutionize treatment of substance use disorder through integrative technology monitoring brain waves in a discreet way that empowers individuals with the tools needed to recover and grow positive mindsets.
